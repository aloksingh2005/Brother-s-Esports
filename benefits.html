B-Coins Reward System

B-Coins are our platform's virtual currency that you can earn through various activities and redeem for exciting rewards.

How to Earn B-Coins:

  • Participating in tournaments (10-50 coins per tournament)
  • Daily login bonus (5 coins per day)
  • Completing special challenges (20-100 coins)
  • Referring friends (50 coins per referral)
  • Watching sponsored ads (2 coins per ad)

Redeeming B-Coins:

  • Tournament entry fees
  • Platform merchandise
  • Mobile game top-ups
  • Gift cards
  • Premium features unlock

Tournament Cashback System

Our unique cashback system rewards your participation in paid tournaments by returning a percentage of your entry fees based on your performance.

Top 10% 20%

Finish in the top 10% of participants and receive 20% of your entry fee back as cashback

Top 25% 15%

Finish in the top 11-25% of participants and receive 15% of your entry fee back as cashback

Top 50% 10%

Finish in the top 26-50% of participants and receive 10% of your entry fee back as cashback

Participation 5%

All participants receive a minimum 5% cashback on their entry fee just for playing

Note: Cashback is credited to your account wallet and can be used for future tournament entries or withdrawn after accumulating ₹100 or more.
