FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I participate in tournaments?

To participate in tournaments, you need to register on our platform, browse available tournaments on the tournaments page, select one that interests you, pay the entry fee if applicable, and follow the instructions for match scheduling and participation.

What is the spectator program and how does it work?

The spectator program allows members to earn rewards by observing tournament matches to ensure fair play. Spectators join matches as observers, report any suspicious activity, and provide match validation. They receive compensation for each match successfully spectated.

How do I upgrade to a premium membership?

You can upgrade to premium by logging into your account, navigating to the membership section in your profile, selecting your preferred plan (Premium or Pro), and completing the payment process. Membership benefits are activated immediately after successful payment.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with the premium features?

Yes, we offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee for new premium memberships. If you're not satisfied with the features, you can request a refund within the first week of your subscription by contacting our support team.

How do I book a coaching session?

To book a coaching session, go to the coaching page, browse available coaches, check their specializations and availability, select a coach that matches your needs, choose a suitable time slot, and complete the booking process. Pro members receive one free session monthly.
