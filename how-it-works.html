FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about our platform and processes

How do I register for tournaments?

After creating an account, go to the Tournaments page, browse available tournaments for your chosen game, and click the Register button. Some tournaments are free to enter, while others may require an entry fee.

What payment methods do you support?

We support UPI, Paytm, major credit/debit cards, and direct bank transfers for both entry fees and reward disbursements. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How do spectators ensure fair play?

Our verified spectators join tournament matches as observers and monitor gameplay in real-time. They can identify and report cheating, teaming in solo modes, and other rule violations. Spectators also collect match statistics for leaderboards.

Can I play in tournaments if I'm not highly skilled?

Absolutely! We host tournaments for all skill levels. Beginner-friendly tournaments are clearly marked, and we also have a matchmaking system that groups players of similar skill levels in certain events.

How long does it take to receive rewards?

Cash rewards are typically processed within 24-48 hours after tournament completion and verification. In-game currency and other digital rewards are usually delivered instantly or within a few hours.
