COMMON ISSUES

Common Account Issues

Can't Login to Account

If you're having trouble logging in:

  1. Make sure you're using the correct mobile number
  2. Try the "Forgot Password" option
  3. Check if your account has been temporarily locked for security
  4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
Still need help?

Mobile Number Verification Issues

If you're not receiving the verification SMS:

  1. Check if your phone has signal and can receive SMS
  2. Make sure you've entered the correct mobile number
  3. Wait a few minutes and request the code again
  4. Check if SMS services are blocked by your carrier
Still need help?

Profile Information Update

To update your profile information:

  1. Login to your account
  2. Go to "My Profile" section
  3. Click on "Edit Profile"
  4. Make your changes and save

Note: Some information may require verification before changes take effect.

Still need help?

Tournament Registration Issues

Can't Register for Tournament

Common reasons you might not be able to register:

  1. Tournament might be full (check participant count)
  2. Registration deadline has passed
  3. Your account level doesn't meet requirements
  4. Payment issues if it's a paid tournament
Still need help?

Team Registration Problems

For team tournament registration issues:

  1. Make sure all team members have active accounts
  2. Check if your team meets the minimum/maximum player requirements
  3. Ensure team captain has permissions to register
  4. Verify all team members have completed their profiles
Still need help?

Tournament Schedule Changes

If your tournament schedule has changed:

  1. Check your registered email for notifications
  2. Login to your account and view tournament details
  3. Follow rescheduling instructions if provided
  4. Contact support if you can't accommodate the new schedule
Still need help?

Payment Issues

Payment Failure

If your payment failed during registration:

  1. Check if you have sufficient balance in your account
  2. Verify your payment method is active and not blocked
  3. Try an alternative payment method
  4. Ensure your bank isn't blocking gaming transactions
Still need help?

Double Payment

If you were charged twice:

  1. Check your bank statement to confirm the double charge
  2. Login to your account and check transaction history
  3. Contact support with transaction details and screenshots
  4. Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days
Contact support

Refund Requests

For tournament entry fee refunds:

  1. Refunds are only available if requested at least 24 hours before tournament
  2. Login and go to "My Tournaments" > "Request Refund"
  3. Fill out the refund request form
  4. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
View Refund Policy

Technical Issues

App Crashing

If the mobile app is crashing:

  1. Update to the latest version of the app
  2. Close all background apps and restart your device
  3. Clear app cache in your device settings
  4. Reinstall the app if issues persist
Still need help?

Website Not Loading

If the website isn't loading properly:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Clear browser cache and cookies
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Disable any ad blockers or extensions that might interfere
Still need help?

Game Connection Issues

If you're having trouble connecting to game servers:

  1. Check your internet connection speed and stability
  2. Verify that your firewall isn't blocking the game
  3. Restart your router/modem
  4. Try connecting via mobile data instead of WiFi (or vice versa)
Still need help?

Game-Specific Issues

Free Fire Custom Room Issues

For problems with Free Fire custom rooms:

  1. Make sure you've entered the correct room ID and password
  2. Check if the room is still active (they expire after a certain time)
  3. Verify your game client is updated to the latest version
  4. If you're hosting, make sure you have the right permissions
Still need help?

BGMI/PUBG Mobile Match Issues

For BGMI tournament matches:

  1. Ensure your device meets the minimum requirements
  2. Check if you have the correct version of the game installed
  3. Make sure you've joined the correct custom room
  4. For lag issues, close background apps and ensure good network connection
Still need help?

COD Mobile Tournament Problems

For Call of Duty Mobile tournaments:

  1. Verify you're using the correct game mode (BR or MP)
  2. Check if you have the right loadouts allowed for the tournament
  3. Make sure you've joined the correct private match
  4. For disconnection issues, check your internet stability
Still need help?

Prize Claim Issues

Prize Not Received

If you haven't received your prize:

  1. Verify your tournament results confirm you're eligible for prizes
  2. Check if you've completed all prize claim requirements
  3. Standard processing time is 3-7 business days
  4. Ensure your payment details are correct in your profile
Still need help?

Payment Method Issues

For problems with prize payment methods:

  1. Update your prize claim method in your profile
  2. Ensure bank details or UPI ID are entered correctly
  3. Some payment methods have minimum amount requirements
  4. Verify your account is KYC compliant if required
Contact support

Team Prize Distribution

For team prize distribution questions:

  1. Team captain needs to complete the prize distribution form
  2. All team members must have completed their profiles
  3. Distribution percentages must be agreed upon by all members
  4. Each member will receive their share separately
Still need help?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Brother's eSports?

Brother's eSports is India's premier mobile gaming platform focused on creating competitive opportunities for players of all skill levels. We host daily tournaments across popular mobile games like Free Fire, BGMI, Call of Duty Mobile, and others, with real cash prizes and rewards.

Was this helpful?

Is Brother's eSports available across all of India?

Yes, Brother's eSports services are available throughout India. Players from any state can register and participate in our tournaments. We host tournaments across different time zones to accommodate players from all regions.

Was this helpful?

What games do you support on your platform?

We currently support popular mobile games including Free Fire, BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India), Call of Duty Mobile, and Clash Royale. We regularly add new games based on popularity and player requests. Check our Games page for the complete and updated list.

Was this helpful?

Do you support PC games or only mobile games?

Currently, Brother's eSports focuses exclusively on mobile gaming tournaments. Our platform is designed specifically for mobile gamers, and we do not support PC games at this time. We're continually evaluating expansion opportunities, so this may change in the future.

Was this helpful?

How do I contact Brother's eSports?

You can contact us through multiple channels:

  • Email: info@brothersesports.com
  • Phone: +91 9876543210 (10 AM - 7 PM IST)
  • Live Chat: Available on our website (9 AM - 9 PM IST)
  • Contact Form: Available on this page
  • Discord: Join our community server

Was this helpful?

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple. Click on the "Register" button on our homepage, enter your mobile number, complete the verification process, and fill in your profile details. The whole process takes less than 2 minutes.

Was this helpful?

How do tournaments work?

Our tournaments follow a structured process. First, you register for a tournament through our platform. Before the match, you'll receive a custom room ID and password. Join the room at the scheduled time and compete against other players. Our spectator system ensures fair play, and winners receive prizes based on their performance.
