Our Mission

At Brother's eSports, our mission is to revolutionize the mobile gaming landscape in India by providing a fair, accessible, and rewarding platform for players of all skill levels.

We aim to foster a thriving competitive environment where talent is recognized and rewarded, while maintaining the highest standards of fair play through our unique spectator system.

By building a community where players can connect, compete, and grow together, we're not just creating a platform – we're shaping the future of mobile eSports in India.

Our Story

Brother's eSports was founded in 2022 by a group of passionate mobile gamers who recognized the immense potential and talent in India's growing mobile gaming community. What started as friendly competitions among friends quickly evolved into a vision for a dedicated platform that could serve the unique needs of mobile gamers across the country.

Frustrated by the lack of fair play in existing tournaments and limited opportunities for mobile players, our founders set out to create a solution that addressed these challenges head-on. After months of development and testing, Brother's eSports was born – a platform specifically designed for mobile gamers, by mobile gamers.

Since our launch, we've grown from hosting small weekly tournaments to becoming one of India's premier mobile eSports platforms, with thousands of registered players and hundreds of tournaments completed. Through it all, we've maintained our core values of fairness, accessibility, and community.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our services, driven by our passion for mobile gaming and our commitment to providing the best possible experience for our players.

Our Values

Fairness

We believe in creating a level playing field where players succeed based on skill, not unfair advantages. Our spectator system ensures integrity in every match.

Community

We're building more than a platform – we're nurturing a community where players connect, learn from each other, and grow together.

Accessibility

We make competitive gaming accessible to everyone, regardless of their experience level or background. Mobile gaming breaks down barriers.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from platform performance to tournament organization and player support.
